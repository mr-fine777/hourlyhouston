About Hourly Houston

Hourly Houston is a local project focused on capturing moments, stories, and observations from across the city. We publish frequent updates and short features that reflect everyday life in Houston — its people, places, and the small events that together shape the community.

Our approach

We prioritize clarity, respect, and timeliness. Content is curated to be immediate and accessible: brief, well-sourced, and grounded in local context. We aim to present a slice of the city without editorial excess — letting the details speak for themselves.

Contribute or get in touch

If you'd like to suggest a story, share a tip, or provide feedback, please reach out using the contact channels listed on our site. We're currently accepting brief submissions and local leads; include a short description and a way to follow up.

Note on sourcing

Where possible, we link to original reporting and public records. Some items are compiled from publicly available sources and on-the-ground reporting; we strive to be transparent about provenance in each post.
